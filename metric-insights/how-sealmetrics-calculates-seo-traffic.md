2. Avoiding Confusion with Google Ads Traffic



Could Google Ads traffic be mistakenly classified as SEO traffic?

The risk is extremely low —less than 1%of cases.

For confusion to happen,bothof the following conditions must be met:

The referrer must still beGoogle(which is typical for Google Ads).ANDthe URL mustlackall identifiable ad-tracking parameters:No gclidNo gad, gbraid, wbraidNo UTM parametersNo other newly introduced Google Ads parameters
