SEO Traffic:The TERM is the landing page URL where the user arrives.Referrer Traffic:The TERM is the URL of the previous page (referrer) that sent the user.Google Ads Traffic:The TERM corresponds to the keyword that triggered the ad.UTM Term Traffic:The TERM is the value you set in the utm_term parameter.
