This video showcases the SealMetrics dashboard. Initially, it displays visits, page views, conversions, billing, and micro-conversions, which will be explained later. The dashboard also features a cost calculator and conversion rate. Additionally, there is a comparison of these KPIs over the last thirty days.​

At the micro-conversion level, it can measure any micro-conversion, such as leads, add-to-cart actions, newsletter sign-ups, logins, checkouts, scrolls, or any other event needed. Similarly, at the conversion level, it can measure conversions as required. The dashboard also provides a comparison with the previous thirty-one days, indicated in green if there is growth or red if there is a decrease.​

Visually, users can track how their business is evolving in terms of conversions and micro-conversions.​

The dashboard can measure any type of event. It includes a graph that shows how traffic is evolving in terms of visits. Users can see which mediums generate the most sales and visits.​

The traffic sources that generate the most visits and conversions are also displayed.

Additionally, the dashboard uses red and green indicators to show whether campaigns are growing or declining. It provides statistics on the most viewed pages, broken down by device, operating system, and country.​

It’s also worth noting that at the page view level, users can dive into the details and see which URLs are capturing the most traffic on entry pages and which ones have the highest number of views.
