Unlike traditional analytics platforms,we do not use IP-based filtering due to privacy regulations.



Instead, we implement two primary filtering methods:



Automatic Bot Detection

Our system automatically filters out all traffic from user agents containing "*bot" in their name. This helps eliminate most known bot traffic from your analytics data.



Behavioural Pattern Filtering

We use an advanced behavioural detection system that identifies suspicious activity patterns:
