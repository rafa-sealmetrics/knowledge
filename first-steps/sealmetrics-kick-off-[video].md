What is Sealmetrics?

Sealmetricsis a privacy-first measurement tool. All data displayed in Sealmetrics is validated by our legal team, ensuring compliance with the most stringent privacy regulations.

Sealmetrics is built on two distinct, consentless measurement systems:

Isolated Hits: Measures each interaction on your website as separate, unconnected events.Session ID: Tracks user sessions without cookies or fingerprinting, creating a temporary session ID that disappears when the user leaves your site.
