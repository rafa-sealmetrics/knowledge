## Step 1: Create Your Account

### Sign Up Process

1. **Visit** [sealmetrics.com/signup](https://sealmetrics.com/signup)
2. **Choose your plan** (Free trial available)
3. **Enter your details**:
   - Business email address
   - Company name
   - Website URL
   - Industry type

<Warning>
Use your business email - personal emails may trigger additional verification steps.
</Warning>

### Account Verification

After signup, you'll receive a verification email:

1. **Check your inbox** (and spam folder)
2. **Click the verification link**
3. **Complete email verification**
4. **Log into your dashboard**

## Step 2: Basic Account Configuration

### Company Information

<Steps>
  <Step title="Business Details">
    - **Company Name**: Your legal business name
    - **Industry**: Select your primary industry
    - **Company Size**: Number of employees
    - **Primary Use Case**: E-commerce, SaaS, Media, etc.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Primary Website">
    - **Website URL**: Your main domain (e.g., https://yoursite.com)
    - **Website Type**: Select the closest match
    - **Expected Traffic**: Monthly pageviews estimate
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Privacy Settings">
    - **Data Location**: Choose your preferred data region
    - **Retention Period**: How long to keep analytics data (default: 25 months)
    - **Privacy Level**: Standard (recommended) or Maximum
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 4: Security Setup

### Two-Factor Authentication (Recommended)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable 2FA">
    Go to **Account Settings** ‚Üí **Security** ‚Üí **Two-Factor Authentication**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Method">
    - **Authenticator App** (recommended): Google Authenticator, Authy
    - **SMS**: Text message verification
    - **Email**: Email-based verification
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Backup Codes">
    - Save your backup codes securely
    - Use them if you lose access to your primary method
    - Generate new codes periodically
  </Step>
</Steps>

### API Keys Setup

If you plan to use our API:

1. **Navigate to** Settings ‚Üí API Keys
2. **Generate new key** with appropriate permissions
3. **Save securely** - keys are only shown once
4. **Set expiration** (recommended: 1 year)

<Warning>
API keys provide full access to your analytics data. Store them securely and rotate regularly.
</Warning>

## Step 6: Initial Website Setup

### Add Your First Website

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Property">
    - Click **"Add Website"** in dashboard
    - Enter your domain (e.g., yoursite.com)
    - Choose website category
    - Set timezone
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Get Tracking Code">
    - Copy your unique tracking script
    - Note your Project ID
    - Download implementation guide
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify Domain">
    - Add DNS TXT record (recommended)
    - Or upload verification file
    - Confirms domain ownership
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Multiple Websites

If you have multiple sites:

- **Add each domain** as separate property
- **Use same account** for centralized billing
- **Set up cross-domain tracking** if needed
- **Configure different team permissions** per site
