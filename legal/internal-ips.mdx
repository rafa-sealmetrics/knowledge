## 

Due to strict data protection regulations and our unwavering commitment to safeguarding personal data, we are sorry to inform you that **we cannot process IP addresses, as they are classified as personal data** under these regulations.

This policy is in place to ensure compliance with data privacy laws and protect all individuals' privacy.

We want you to know that we prioritize data privacy and security in all our operations. We continuously work on improving our systems to provide the best possible service while strictly adhering to privacy standards. I greatly appreciate your understanding and cooperation in this matter.
