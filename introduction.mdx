Sealmetrics is the **only analytics solution that captures 100% of your traffic** while maintaining complete privacy compliance. No cookies, no consent banners, no data loss.

- **100% Traffic Visibility**: See all visitors, not just those who accept cookies
- **Real-Time Analytics**: Instant data availability, no 24-48 hour delays
- **No Data Sampling**: Every visitor and conversion counted accurately
- **Ad-Blocker Proof**: Unaffected by privacy tools and browser restrictions

- **GDPR/CCPA Compliant**: No consent required, legally bulletproof
- **No Personal Data**: Zero individual tracking or profiling
