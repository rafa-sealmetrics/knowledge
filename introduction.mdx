## Why Sealmetrics?

<Accordion>
  <AccordionItem title="🆚 Sealmetrics vs Google Analytics 4">
    - **Data Loss**: GA4 loses 30-60% of data due to consent requirements
    - **Privacy Compliance**: GA4 requires complex consent management
    - **Data Accuracy**: GA4 uses sampling, we capture 100% of data
    - **Legal Safety**: GA4 faces ongoing legal challenges in EU
  </AccordionItem>
  
  <AccordionItem title="🔒 Privacy-First Philosophy">
